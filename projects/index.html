Zoned Out

"Zoned Out" was a project where I decided to recreate the backrooms in a couple of months, inspired by the already existing "Escape the Backrooms" by Fancy, somehow I believed I could make a fully functional first person multiplayer horror experience with NO prior experience to multiplayer and it being my 5th project ever made in Unity, released December 9th 2023.

nextbot mayhem

"nextbot mayhem" was a recreation of the roblox game "Nico's Nextbots" as during the time, the game was taken down due to roblox's moderation. Unfortunately, I wasn't able to finish the game in time as the game came back up only DAYS later whereas this project took about 2 and a half months. My community enjoyed this game the most out of every project and wanted a multiplayer version, however nextbot mayhem 2 was never officially made as the original already had countless bugs and none of the code or models were sorted neatly and were very performance heavy on the user. Released October 7th 2023.

The Follower

"The Follower" was a short 2 week project made by me after I got challenged by a couple of friends to make a horror game within a 2 week time period. I took on this challenge and it's still the biggest project on my channel currently having over 19,000 views to date. Released August 12th 2023.

Big Bills

"Big Bills" is my first ever released project at the beginning of learning Unity. The project took the longest out of any project I have made so far (from all my old projects) starting its creation as far back as March 2023, taking about 4-5 months to create and polish. My community loved the sily name "Big Bills" and took it as a funny joke that has stuck around since it's release. At the time, the game was impressive for somebody who had barely touched unity. Released July 15th 2023.
