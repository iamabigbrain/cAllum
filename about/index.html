About

Hey, I'm cAllum (formerly known as BigBrain), and I am an indie game developer who's trying to grow with fun little projects that get worked on. I hope for this to be my future job one day and show people what I can really make. Although my projects are small, basic and old, I still strive to make high-quality indie games that can be enjoyed by many.

I started at the young age of 7, starting with small projects on Scratch that I used to show to my class in primary (first) school with games such as platformers and some tiny skits made within the app. At the age of 10, I moved to Roblox Studio where I would make short unreleased animations and unreleased games. Over time, I tried all different kinds of hobbies/niches such as music production, graphic design, animation, game development, design and more. Although I quit for a period of time to pursue school in Q2 of 2024 to complete exams, it's still a BIG dream to come back and work on more projects.

I always want to learn new things whenever and wherever I can to help grow my understanding.

You can find me on YouTube or Contact me!
